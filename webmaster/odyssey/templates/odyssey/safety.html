Space travel is rigorous-- even for seasoned astronauts. So, we've prepared a 2-month long training regime to get you ready for take off.

After you book your tour, you will be asked to submit a preliminary physical exam. We will be looking for these specific things: 20/20 vision (with or without correction lenses), a blood pressure less than 140/90 while sitting down, and a height between 157.5 cm and 190.5 cm.

Once you've passed your physical, it's time to officially your start your Odyssey. You will be fitted into your very own custom spacesuit, complete with personal adornments.

Now that your spacesuit is done, it's time to train your body and mind. For the next 22 days, we will create a balanced diet for you to ensure your top health. Every day will be different and tougher than the previous day, but we believe in you!

You've passed the first stage of training. Now, it's finally time for you to put that spacesuit to use. Slowly but surely, we will train you to be able to swim 3 lengths of an Olympic swimming pool in your spacesuit.

After a hard few weeks, you'll get to know your pilots and the rest of your spacecrew through some team-building exercises!

Next, you'll spend the next few days training in our specialized facilities, where we will acclimatize you to the heavy G-forces during travel.

As you continue your daily training, you will be taught how to put on your spacesuit and of the many functions it has.

It's a routine for astronauts to be quarantined two weeks before launch, to make sure that you are as healthy as you can be when you travel to space.

Today is when you will take a full-length physical exam to guarantee your health during space travel.

The day has finally arrived. Off you go to your Odyssey!



INTRODUCING

THE NAUSICA

You will feel right at home in our cockpit, courtesy of our minimalistic styling and expansive layout. The Nausica was designed with the latest technologies to ensure your safety and comfort in your odyssey to space.

YOU IN MIND

The Nausica's seats were built with memory foam to ensure maximum comfort during space flight. We have employed world-class ergonomists to ensure the absolute best seating position.

In addition to our comfortable seats, we provide your helmets with crisp microphones and speakers to assure constant communication between you, your crewmates, and the ground crew.


WE GUARANTEE

YOUR SAFETY

Our seatbelts were built by top engineers with state-of-the-art technology to ensure maximum comfort and safety during flight. Your safety and comfort is our promise.

The outer layer of the Nausica is reinforced with the latest heat shield technology to protect you from the intense heat of spaceflight. Worry not, we guarantee that you will not break a single sweat during your journey.





LAUNCH AND RECOVERY

To keep the Odyssey's promise, we have incorporated a complex launch and recovery system powered by our latest technologies. After all, Odyssey will stand by your side in your journey to space.



Odyssey's top engineers are there to walk you towards the Nausica and ensure that all systems are good for launch. Our propellants are built with only the best materials and top-quality fuel to ensure a smooth journey and release.

The Nausica's engine will separate three minutes into the flight, and the Nausica capsule will separate from its second stage. From this point, our pilots will make contact with Odyssey headquarters and check all systems. On the third day of orbit, the Nausica will open its thrusters to bring you towards your destination.

After you've finished your odyssey in space, the Nausica will end off your adventure with a splash in the Pacific Ocean. Our capsules are made with durable heat shields that protects its interior from the intense temperatures of re-entry.Thanks to our cutting-edge navigation systems, our recovery crew can quickly track the spacecraft's location and bring you home safely.

Oddysey's recovery crews will take back the main capsule, which will be reused for the next flight. Before taking it in for maintenance, we provide you an opportunity to take pictures in front of the capsule to mark the end of your journey.
