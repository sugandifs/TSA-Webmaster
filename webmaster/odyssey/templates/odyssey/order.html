FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



How will payments work?

$10,000 will incur as a surcharge, and after the tickets are redeemed.


Why is there a surcharge of $10,000 for the orbital tour?
The surcharge of $10,000 is applied to cover the additional costs associated with providing an orbital tour experience. Unlike regular space tourism flights, which operate at suborbital altitudes, an orbital tour takes you into Earth's orbit, allowing you to experience weightlessness and witness the curvature of our planet from space. The technology, resources, and infrastructure required for such a mission are significantly more complex and expensive, hence the surcharge is necessary to cover these extraordinary expenses.


When do I need to pay the surcharge?
The surcharge of $10,000 is due after you have successfully redeemed your tickets and confirmed your reservation for the orbital tour. At the time of booking, you will be required to pay the base cost of the tour, and the surcharge amount will be invoiced separately to ensure clarity and transparency in the payment process.

Can I go on more than 1 tour at the same time?

Odyssey offers an all-inclusive deluxe tour package that covers all three of our tours. No, it is not possible to go on more than one tour at the same time. Each orbital tour requires specific planning, resources, and coordination to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. The logistics involved in conducting an orbital tour are intricate and demand careful attention to detail. Therefore, it is not feasible to participate in multiple tours simultaneously. However, if you are interested in additional tours, our team will be happy to assist you in planning future experiences after completing your initial tour.

Are children allowed to go on a tour?

Due to the rigor of spaceflight, we are limiting our tours to adults 18 years and older.
While we understand the excitement and curiosity that children may have about space travel, the safety and well-being of our passengers are our top priorities. Spaceflight, particularly orbital tours, involves certain physical and physiological challenges that require a high level of physical fitness, maturity, and understanding.


The rigorous conditions of space travel, including the forces experienced during launch and reentry, the potential for weightlessness and readjustment to Earth's gravity, and the complex protocols and procedures that need to be followed, make it necessary to restrict participation to adults. These measures help ensure the safety and comfort of all passengers aboard our spacecraft.


However, we encourage younger space enthusiasts to continue nurturing their interest in space exploration and look forward to a time when space travel experiences can be safely extended to a wider age range. In the meantime, we strive to inspire and engage younger audiences through educational programs and outreach initiatives, sparking their curiosity and fostering a passion for space science and exploration.


Please note that our age limit policy is subject to change in the future as technology advances and safety standards evolve. We recommend staying updated on our website or contacting our customer service for any updates or changes to our age restrictions.

How do I redeem my tickets?

Present your ticket information as well as your security question response in your profile center as you board the flight and an Odysseynaut will change the status for you!
