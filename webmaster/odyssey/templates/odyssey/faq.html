Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there a surcharge of $10,000 for the orbital tour?
Payments

The surcharge of $10,000 is applied to cover the additional costs associated with providing an orbital tour experience. Unlike regular space tourism flights, which operate at suborbital altitudes, an orbital tour takes you into Earth's orbit, allowing you to experience weightlessness and witness the curvature of our planet from space. The technology, resources, and infrastructure required for such a mission are significantly more complex and expensive, hence the surcharge is necessary to cover these extraordinary expenses.

When do I need to pay the surcharge?
Payments

The surcharge of $10,000 is due after you have successfully redeemed your tickets and confirmed your reservation for the orbital tour. At the time of booking, you will be required to pay the base cost of the tour, and the surcharge amount will be invoiced separately to ensure clarity and transparency in the payment process.

Can I pay the surcharge in installments?
Payments

We understand that the surcharge amount is substantial, and we want to make it feasible for our customers. As such, we offer the option to pay the surcharge in installments, provided that the full amount is paid before the departure date of your orbital tour. Our team will be happy to discuss a payment plan that suits your needs and ensures a smooth payment process.

What does the surcharge cover?
Payments

The surcharge covers various additional expenses associated with an orbital tour, including advanced life support systems, extended duration in space, specialized training for the crew, enhanced safety measures, and the intricate engineering required for orbital maneuvers. These aspects contribute to the overall complexity and uniqueness of the orbital tour experience.

Can I go on more than one tour at the same time?
Tours

Unfortunately, it is not possible to go on more than one orbital tour at the same time. Each orbital tour is meticulously planned and scheduled to ensure the safety and enjoyment of all participants. The complexities involved in orchestrating an orbital tour require careful coordination of resources, crew, and spacecraft availability. Therefore, it is not feasible to accommodate multiple tours simultaneously for a single individual.
However, if you are interested in experiencing multiple orbital tours, you can certainly book additional tours on separate occasions, subject to availability. Each tour offers a unique perspective and set of experiences, allowing you to further explore the wonders of space and gain a deeper understanding of our universe.

Are there any age restrictions for participating in an orbital tour?
Tours

Yes, there are age restrictions for participating in an orbital tour. Due to the physical and physiological demands of space travel, participants must meet certain age criteria to ensure their safety and well-being during the tour. The minimum age requirement for an orbital tour is typically set at 18 years old. This age restriction helps to ensure that participants have reached a level of physical maturity that can better withstand the unique conditions of space travel.
Additionally, there may be an upper age limit, usually set at around 70 years old, to account for potential health considerations and the overall fitness required for the journey. However, the specific age restrictions may vary depending on the space tourism provider and the requirements set by the regulatory authorities.

What is the duration of an orbital tour?
Tour Duration

The duration of an orbital tour can vary depending on the specific tour package. Generally, an orbital tour lasts anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks, including the time spent in space and necessary preparations before and after the journey.

What are the physical requirements for an orbital tour?
Physical Fitness

Participating in an orbital tour requires a certain level of physical fitness. Prior to the tour, participants are required to undergo thorough medical evaluations and fitness assessments to ensure their ability to withstand the physical demands of space travel.

What safety measures are in place during an orbital tour?
Safety Precautions

Safety is of utmost importance during an orbital tour. Stringent safety protocols are followed, including comprehensive training for the crew, advanced life support systems, emergency procedures, and thorough spacecraft inspections to ensure a secure and enjoyable journey.

What is the weightlessness experience like during an orbital tour?
Weightlessness Experience

During an orbital tour, you will experience the incredible sensation of weightlessness. In the absence of gravity, you will float freely inside the spacecraft, allowing you to move and navigate without the usual constraints of Earth's gravity.

What kind of training do participants receive before an orbital tour?
Pre-Tour Training

Participants undergo comprehensive training before an orbital tour. This includes classroom sessions, simulations, physical exercises, and familiarization with spacecraft systems to ensure that they are well-prepared for the unique challenges and experiences of space travel.

Can I bring personal belongings on an orbital tour?
Personal Belongings

While there are limitations on the size and weight of personal belongings you can bring on an orbital tour, you are typically allowed to bring essential items such as clothing, toiletries, and personal mementos. However, it's important to follow the guidelines provided by the space tourism provider to ensure safety and compliance with spaceflight regulations.

Are there any specific health requirements for participating in an orbital tour?
Health Requirements

Participants in an orbital tour are generally required to be in good health. Pre-existing medical conditions and certain health factors may affect your eligibility for the tour. It is important to consult with the space tourism provider and undergo medical evaluations to ensure that you meet the necessary health requirements.

What is the best time of year to go on an orbital tour?
Ideal Tour Timing

The best time of year for an orbital tour can vary depending on various factors, including weather conditions, celestial events, and space mission schedules. How do I prepare for the physical demands of an orbital tour?
Physical Preparation

Preparing for the physical demands of an orbital tour requires regular exercise, strength training, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. It is advisable to consult with a healthcare professional or a qualified fitness trainer who can provide guidance on specific exercises and preparations to ensure you are physically ready for the journey.

What happens if there is a medical emergency during an orbital tour?
Medical Emergencies

Space tourism companies have stringent protocols in place to handle medical emergencies during an orbital tour. The crew is trained in first aid and emergency medical procedures, and the spacecraft is equipped with medical supplies and equipment to address potential health issues that may arise while in space.

Can I communicate with people on Earth during an orbital tour?
Communication with Earth

Yes, participants on an orbital tour can communicate with people on Earth. The spacecraft is equipped with communication systems that allow for real-time communication through various channels, such as voice and video calls. However, communication may be subject to certain limitations depending on the specific mission parameters and technical capabilities.
