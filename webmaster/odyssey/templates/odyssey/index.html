01 | Training



Space travel is rigorous-- even for seasoned astronauts. So, we've prepared a 2-month long training regime to get you ready for take off. Regardless of what tour you chose, our training regime is all included in our tour packages.

After you book your tour, you will be asked to submit a preliminary physical exam. We will be looking for these specific things: 20/20 vision (with or without correction lenses), a blood pressure less than 140/90 while sitting down, and a height between 157.5 cm and 190.5 cm.

Once you've passed your physical, it's time to officially your start your Odyssey. You will be fitted into your very own custom spacesuit, complete with personal adornments.

Now that your spacesuit is done, it's time to train your body and mind. For the next 22 days, we will create a balanced diet for you to ensure your top health. Every day will be different and tougher than the previous day, but we believe in you!

You've passed the first stage of training. Now, it's finally time for you to put that spacesuit to use. Slowly but surely, we will train you to be able to swim 3 lengths of an Olympic swimming pool in your spacesuit.

After a hard few weeks, you'll get to know your pilots and the rest of your spacecrew through some team-building exercises!

Next, you'll spend the next few days training in our specialized facilities, where we will acclimatize you to the heavy G-forces during travel.

As you continue your daily training, you will be taught how to put on your spacesuit and of the many functions it has.

It's a routine for astronauts to be quarantined two weeks before launch, to make sure that you are as healthy as you can be when you travel to space.

Today is when you will take a full-length physical exam to guarantee your health during space travel.

The day has finally arrived. Off you go to your Odyssey!
