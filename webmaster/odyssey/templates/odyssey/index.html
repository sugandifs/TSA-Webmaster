FIND YOUR ODYSSEY


$10k ORBITAL TOUR

The Orbital Tour will bring you on board the Nausica to get a captivating view of the Earth, Moon, and its nearby planets. With the large windows of the Nausica, we guarantee that every minute of this trip will be filled with astounding views.

$10k ISS TOUR

The ISS Tour will take you on board the International Space Station and experience what it's like to be an astronaut. Our knowledgeable pilots will provide you with a guided tour of the ISS before orbitting around the moon and returning back home to Earth.

$20k MOON TOUR

The Moon Tour will take you to the moon and have some fun in zero gravity. Odyssey's pilots will give you a guided tour about moon rocks and other cool facts about the moon. On your way home, you will have the opportunity to orbit the Earth and its nearby planets.
