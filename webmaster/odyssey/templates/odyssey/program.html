  • D-21 TO LAUNCH

    In order to ensure that you are prepared for the rigor of spaceflight, we require you to take a standard physical exam at our headquarters. We will be looking for:

    1: 20/20 vision with or without correction lenses

    2: 140/90 or less blood pressure in a seating position
